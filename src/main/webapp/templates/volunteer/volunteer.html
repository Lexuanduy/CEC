Volunteer at CEC

We welcome volunteers at CEC (Crazy English Community) to help our students and organization. We need volunteers to help:

Teaching English.

Learning English is part of our course and it will be very helpful for CEC students when volunteers help with English speaking practice. When volunteers work with teachers, there will be plenty of time to practice in small groups.

- Requirements:

  • Speaks good english.
  • Commit to at least one morning or afternoon every week for at least one month.
  • Work with the English teacher and match the lesson plan of the class.
  • Helps to improve textbooks and materials in English.

Other work in our organization.

We also welcome volunteers to give a helping hand to our administrative, marketing, and event organizing teams.

- Requirements:

  • Commit to a minimum of 1 month (flexible working hours)
  • Enthusiastic and flexible.
  • Speaks English / Vietnamese.
